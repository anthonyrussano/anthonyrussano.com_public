Lilac Sage (Salvia verticillata)

Composition

The essential oil contains monoterpenes (1,8-cineole, α-pinene, camphor, geraniol, linalool and Υ-terpinene).[1]

Healing Properties

Anticonvulsant

(anti-seizure) extract of S. verticillata resulted in a significant anticonvulsant activity.[1:1]

Antidepressant

Extract of S. verticillata resulted in a significant antidepressant effect.[1:2]

Disease / Symptom Treatment
